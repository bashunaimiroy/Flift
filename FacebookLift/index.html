How It Works

FacebookLift's machine learning algorithms read your public posts and get a feeling for how you speak. After that, the app will ask you to approve or disapprove of a number of procedurally generated posts. When you think it's making the right choices to represent you, turn on Auto-Pilot and watch it populate your profile!
